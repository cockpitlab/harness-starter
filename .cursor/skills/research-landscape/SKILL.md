# Skill: Research Landscape

**Description:** Performs structured landscape reviews, trend analysis, and pain-point identification without assuming integration.

**When to use:**
- When the human asks exploratory questions about new tools or approaches
- When scanning for emerging best practices
- When the human says "explore", "landscape review", or "what's new in..."

**Behavior:**
- Always treat as information gathering only
- Never propose adding the tool to the project unless explicitly asked
- Present findings clearly in `docs/references/` or `docs/exec-plans/`
- State: "This is exploratory research only — no changes to the project are proposed"
